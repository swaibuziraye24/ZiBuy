🌍 Who We Are

ZiBuy is Uganda's fast-growing online classifieds marketplace, designed to make buying and selling as easy, safe, and accessible as possible for every Ugandan — whether you're in Kampala, Gulu, Mbarara, or anywhere across the country.

We believe everyone deserves access to a fair, transparent, and trustworthy marketplace. Whether you're a small business owner, an individual seller clearing out your home, or a buyer looking for the best deal, ZiBuy is built for you.

Our platform supports phones, electronics, fashion, vehicles, real estate services, agriculture, and much more — everything Ugandans need, in one place.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower every Ugandan with a free, safe, and professional platform to grow their business and find great deals — connecting communities through commerce.
