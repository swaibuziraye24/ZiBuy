❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post an ad on ZiBuy?

Click the "+ Post Ad" button in the top navigation bar. Follow the 4-step process: choose your category, fill in item details, upload photos, and review before publishing. Basic posting is completely free.

How do I get my seller verification badge?

Go to Dashboard → Get Verified. Upload your National ID and business documents, pay the one-time UGX 10,000 fee via MTN or Airtel Money, and submit. Our team reviews within 24-48 hours.

How does ad boosting work?

Boosted ads appear at the top of search results with a ⭐ Featured badge. Pay via MTN (Merchant Code: 27868095) or Airtel (+256575996624), then submit your transaction reference. Boost is activated within 1 hour after payment verification.

I paid but my boost/plan wasn't activated. What do I do?

Contact us on WhatsApp at +256 790 548 910 with your payment reference code and transaction ID. Our admin will verify and activate within 1 hour during business hours.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Dashboard → Settings → Danger Zone → Delete My Account. You'll need to confirm with your password. All your data and listings will be permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone.

Is ZiBuy available as a mobile app?

Yes! ZiBuy is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Open ZiBuy in your mobile browser and tap "Install ZiBuy" or "Add to Home Screen" to install it like a native app with offline access.

How do I report a scam or suspicious listing?

Visit our Report Abuse page or contact us directly on WhatsApp. Include the listing URL and as much detail as possible. We investigate all reports within 24 hours.

What payment methods does ZiBuy accept?

For premium services (boosts, plans, verification), we accept MTN Mobile Money and Airtel Money. For purchases between buyers and sellers, ZiBuy recommends cash on delivery as the safest method.
